# Catalog & Samples

[!INCLUDE [preview banner](includes/sl-important-info.md)]

The Studio Live catalog offers a curated set of samples and starter templates. Each entry is a complete, working Uno Platform application you can open, explore, and customize immediately.

## Browsing the Catalog

1. On the Studio Live home screen, click **Browse Catalog**.
2. Use the search box or category filters to narrow the results.
3. Click a sample card to see a description and a preview screenshot.
4. Click **Open** to load the sample into Studio Live.

## Opening a Sample

When you open a sample, Studio Live:

1. Clones the sample project into a fresh workspace.
2. Builds and starts the live preview.
3. Opens the conversation panel so you can immediately start customizing.

The sample is your own copy — any changes you make do not affect the original.

## Exporting a Sample

To save your customized sample locally:

1. Click the **Export** button in the toolbar.
2. A `.zip` of the full Uno Platform solution is downloaded.
3. Open it in Visual Studio or Rider to continue development.
