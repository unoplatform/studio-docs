# Getting Started with Studio Live

[!INCLUDE [preview banner](includes/sl-important-info.md)]

Studio Live runs entirely in the browser — no local installation required.

## Prerequisites

- A modern browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox recommended).
- An Uno Platform account. [Sign up for free](https://platform.uno/studio) if you don't have one.

## First Launch

1. Navigate to [studio.platform.uno](https://studio.platform.uno).
2. Sign in with your Uno Platform account.
3. The home screen shows the <xref:Uno.StudioLive.CatalogSamples> and your recent projects.

## Starting From a Sample

The fastest way to get going is to pick a sample from the catalog:

1. On the home screen, click **Browse Catalog**.
2. Select a sample that matches your use case (e.g., *Counter*, *Todo List*, *Dashboard*).
3. Click **Open** — Studio Live loads the sample, compiles it, and shows a live preview.

See <xref:Uno.StudioLive.CatalogSamples> for the full catalog reference.

## Starting From Scratch

1. On the home screen, click **New Project**.
2. Choose a base template (e.g., *Blank*, *Material Starter*).
3. Enter a project name and click **Create**.
4. The AI agent introduces itself in the conversation panel — describe what you want to build.

## Exporting Your Project

When you are ready to continue locally:

1. Click the **Export** button in the toolbar.
2. A `.zip` file containing the full Uno Platform solution is downloaded.
3. Open the solution in Visual Studio or Rider to continue development.
